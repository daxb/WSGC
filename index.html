About the Club

Established in 1940,
for the West Seattle Golf Community

The West Seattle Golf Club was founded by a group of passionate local golfers who wanted more than just a round of golf — they wanted to build community. What started as informal matches and tournaments has grown into one of Seattle's most active and welcoming golf clubs.

We are not affiliated with the course management. We are the players — organizing tournaments, building friendships, and keeping the competitive spirit alive at West Seattle Golf Course. Whether you're a scratch golfer or a new to the game, WSGC is your home course community.

The Course

West Seattle Golf Course

A historic public course with views of the Puget Sound, Mt Rainier, and the Seattle skyline. Managed by Premier Golf and owned by the City of Seattle, this is where we play.

Visit Course Website
01

A Course with History

Originally established in 1940, West Seattle Golf Course has been a beloved fixture of the community for over eight decades. While the course and the club has evolved, our welcoming character remains.

02

Views Like No Other

Few municipal courses in the country offer panoramic views of snow-capped mountains and open water. Several holes play along ridgelines with stunning sightlines in every direction.

03

Public Access

The course is publicly owned and operated by Premier Golf. WSGC members enjoy the same access as all players, enhanced by our organized events and community.

04

Par 72 Challenge

At over 6,600 yards from the tips, West Seattle is a legitimate test of golf. Tight tree-lined fairways, undulating greens, and strategic bunkering reward smart play.

05

Year-Round Play

Thanks to the Pacific Northwest's mild climate, the course stays open nearly every day of the year. Winter golf is real — and WSGC members embrace it fully.

06

Community First

This isn't a country club course — it's a people's course. And that's exactly what makes it special. WSGC brings the club experience to public golf.

* Course details are subject to input from club historians. Have info to share? Reach out to the board.

Membership

Join the Club

Becoming a member of WSGC means joining a community of golfers who take the game seriously — and themselves not so much.

Open to Everyone

Membership is open to everyone who loves golf. Join or renew for 2026 and become part of a welcoming community of golfers at West Seattle Golf Course.

What You Get

Eligibility to play in all WSGC events. A USGA Handicap Index through the GHIN system. Membership with WA Golf and the Pacific Northwest Golf Association — including member rates at The Home Course, Chambers Bay, OGA Golf Club, and Poppy Hills GC. Plus complimentary access to WA Golf Rewards, WAgolfpass.com, Cutter & Buck member pricing, and a subscription to Pacific Northwest Golfer Magazine.

Renewing Members

You'll need your GHIN number to renew. If you've forgotten it, contact us before registering and we'll look it up for you. New members will be assigned a GHIN number upon joining.

Bylaws & Expectations

We operate under a simple set of bylaws that govern tournament play, membership standards, and board elections. All members are expected to maintain proper pace of play, good sportsmanship, and respect for the course and fellow players.
